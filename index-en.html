    • About me

    I am pleased to welcome you to my website! My name is Markus Kampitsch and I have been working professionally in the field of hydrogen for more than 15 years.

    As a project manager for hydrogen tank development, refueling technology, and refueling stations at BMW and later Great Wall Motor in China, I was responsible as a so-called qualified person for the areas of testing, vehicle integration, plant safety & operation, hydrogen logistics, and the associated organizational measures.

    Since founding KEI GmbH in 2019, I have been working as a consultant in the field of hydrogen technology and business development, supporting international SMEs, start-ups, and investors in the areas of technology assessment and development, technical due diligence, M&A, safety analysis (HAZOP), as well as the evaluation of innovative hybrid energy supply systems and their associated business models.

    My current focus areas are the storage, transport, and refueling of compressed hydrogen and cryo-compressed hydrogen (CcH2).

    My focus is always on pragmatic solutions that are both technically and economically convincing.

    I am a proud sponsor of ISO TC197 and CSA 4.1/4.9
