About me

My name is Markus Kampitsch and I have been working full-time in the field of hydrogen for over 15 years. As a project manager for Hydrogen tank development, refuelling technology and filling stations at BMW and later Great Wall Motor in China, I managed the areas of testing, vehicle integration, plant safety & operation, hydrogen logistics and the associated organizational measures as a so-called qualified person. With the foundation of KEI GmbH in 2019, I started my own business as a consultant in the field of hydrogen technology and business development and since then I have been supporting international SMEs, start-ups and investors in the areas of technology assessment and development, technical due diligence, M&A, safety analyses (HAZOP) and the evaluation of innovative hybrid energy supply systems and the associated business models. My current focus is on the storage, transportation and refuelling of compressed hydrogen and cryogenic compressed hydrogen (CcH2). My focus is always on pragmatic solutions that are technically and economically convincing!

I am a proud sponsor of ISO TC197 and CSA 4.1/4.9
