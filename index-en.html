About me

I am pleased to welcome you to my website! My name is Markus Kampitsch and I have been working professionally in the field of hydrogen for over 15 years.

As a project manager for hydrogen tank development, refueling technology, and hydrogen refueling stations at BMW and later at Great Wall Motor in China, I was responsible, as a certified expert, for the areas of testing, vehicle integration, system safety & operations, hydrogen logistics, and the associated organizational measures.

Through my PhD. in the field of jet engine simluation gained at the University of German Armed Forces and as a developer and project manager for system simulation of space and aviation propulsion systems at Airbus Military and IABG in my earlier carreer, I also have a sound background in related applications as well as performance and operational challenges associated with using hydrogen as a fuel for airborne systems.

Since founding KEI GmbH in 2019, I have been working as a consultant in the fields of hydrogen technology and business development, supporting international SMEs, start-ups, and investors in technology assessment and development, technical due diligence, M&A, safety analyses, as well as in the evaluation of innovative hybrid energy supply systems and their business models.

My current focus areas are storage, transport, and refueling of compressed hydrogen and cryogenic compressed hydrogen (CcH2).

My focus is always on pragmatic solutions that are both technically and economically convincing.
